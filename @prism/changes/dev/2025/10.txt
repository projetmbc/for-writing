:new:
-----

[[:doc:]]
\Pdf \doc: automatic integration of \prod \doc in ext::''MD'' format into the \latex \doc.


--------
:update:
--------

[[:api:]]
\luadraw \prod: the ''getPal'' \func has a new optional \arg ''options'' which is a \dict like array. Here are the keys added.

    + The ''extract'' key, with a list of non-zero integers, extracts some colors from the palette without changing their order.

    + The ''reverse'' key, with a boolean value, reverses the order of the palette.

    + The ''shift'' key, with an integer value, is used to shift the colors in the palette in a circular fashion.
