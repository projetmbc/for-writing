[[:api:]]
Multiple \distrib formats are now available to meet various technical requirements.

    1) High-fidelity (unmodified).

    1) Normalized (''size <= AUTO_QUAL_CATEGO_SIZE'').

    1) Modular (one file per palette).

    1) Monolithic (all palettes in one file).
