-------
:break:
-------

[[:api:]]
Two breaking chnages.

    1) \Prods: \luadraw becomes \lua.

    1) \lua product: removed dict-like \var lua::''palNames''. Therefore, the responsibility of adding this specific \var will lie with \luadraw.


-----
:new:
-----

[[:api:]]
The new features are as follows.

    1) Added \css and \latex products.

    1) Added palettes from the \palettable \proj.

    1) Added three semantic palettes ''TdocBW'', ''TdocCol'' and ''TdocDark'' made for \tutodoc using the \latex \prod.


[[:doc:]]
A \demo is available online (the showcase of the \css \prod is used).

:update:

[[:doc:]]

    1) Manual.

        + Added lists of renamed and removed palettes.

    1) Better palette references.

    1) Better showcases.
