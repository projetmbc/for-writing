Here are the breaking \chges.

    1) Palettes: all final palettes now consist of 10 colors.

    1) \luadraw \prod: the lua::''getPal'' \dict array converted to \func accepting string palette names (with or without lua::''pal'' prefix).


-----
:new:
-----

[[:api:]]
Here are the new features.

    1) Palettes.

        + Added  ''Lemon'' and ''ShiftRainbow'' palettes (\luadraw creation process).

        + Added 37 palettes from the project \scicolmap.

    1)  \luadraw \prod: the lua::''getPal'' \func has an optional \arg lua::''options'' (dict-like array).

        + lua::''extract'' key to give a list of non-zero integers to extract specific colors (preserves order).

        + lua::''reverse'' key to give a boolean to reverse palette color order, or not (lua::''false'' by defaul)

        + lua::''shift'' key to give an integer for circular color shift.


[[:doc:]]
Documentations.

    + Added \pdf manual.

    + Showcase: two \pdf files demonstrating each palette (white and dark modes).
