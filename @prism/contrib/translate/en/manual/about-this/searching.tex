\section{How to choose a palette?}

Here are a few tips to help you select your color palette.
%
\begin{enumerate}
   \item An \thisdemo%
   \footnote{
       \thisdemourl
   }
   proposes an intuitive way to look for a palettes with a random button to discover unknown palettes.
   
   \item Appendix \ref{palette-categos} (page \pageref{palette-categos}) presents all palettes, organized by category, along with a visualization of their continuous spectra (for all), and their corresponding discrete palette definitions (only if size $\leq$ \palMaxSemSize).
\end{enumerate}
