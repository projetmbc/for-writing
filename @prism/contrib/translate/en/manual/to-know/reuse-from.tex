\section{Reuse from...}

Here are the key points to remember when using palettes similar to those offered by projects listed in the section \ref{projects-used}.
%
\begin{enumerate}
	\item 
	\thisproj\ uses standardized \verb+CamelCase+ notation. Therefore, palette names such as \tdoccodein{py}{berlin} and \tdoccodein{py}{gist_heat} become \tdoccodein{text}{Berlin} and \tdoccodein{text}{GistHeat} respectively.
    
    \item \matplotlib\ palettes with a name ending with the \verb+_r+ suffix (reversed color order) are not included in \thisproj.
\end{enumerate}


\begin{tdocnote}
    Most \thisproj\ implementations provide methods to easily obtain reversed palettes, sub-palettes, and color-shifted palettes.
    See the section \ref{products-all}.
\end{tdocnote}


\begin{tdoccaut}
    Most \thisproj\ implementations add the \verb+pal+ prefix to standardized \verb+CamelCase+ names.
    See the section \ref{products-all}.
\end{tdoccaut}
