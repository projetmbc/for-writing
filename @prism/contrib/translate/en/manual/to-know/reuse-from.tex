\subsection{Matplotlib}

Here are the key points to remember when using a palette similar to those offered by \matplotlib.
%
\begin{enumerate}
	\item \label{reuse-std-naming}
	\thisproj\ uses standardized \verb+CamelCase+ notation. Therefore, \matplotlib\ palette names such as \tdoccodein{py}{berlin} and \tdoccodein{py}{gist_heat} become \tdoccodein{text}{Berlin} and \tdoccodein{text}{GistHeat} respectively.
    
    \item All names ending with the \verb+_r+ suffix correspond to reversed color order. These palettes are not included in the \thisproj\ project. However, the implementations provide methods to easily obtain reversed palettes, sub-palettes, and color-shifted palettes.
\end{enumerate}
